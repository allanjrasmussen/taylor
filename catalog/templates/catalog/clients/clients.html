Create your customers with their measurements in your customer records and get their measurements directly into your instructions. Once you have created a customer you will see a complete list of the measurements being used in the instructions on the basic blocks. You can choose to take all of the measurements of your customer and enter them in the table right away, or you can wait to take the measurements until you need them for a particular basic block. Some of the measurements appear in several instructions, but when you have entered a measurement it is stored in the table in the customer records and you should only take the same measurement once.

Click on the question mark if you are in doubt about how the various measurements is taken, or see the full instruction in the menu Studio How to take measurements
