Here is a complete list of the measurements that are being used in the instructions for the basic blocks. You can choose to get all your measurements taken and write them into the table right away, but you can also wait to get your measurements taken until you need them for a particular basic block. Several of the measurements appear in several of the instructions, but when you have entered a measurement it is stored in the table and you should only take the same measurement once. Remember to get another person to take your measurements according to our instructions for taking measurements.
